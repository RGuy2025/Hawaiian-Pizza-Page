Hawaiian Pizza is a Canadian created pizza that includes tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, ham and/or bacon and pineapple. It was created at the Satellite Family Restaurant in Chatham, ON, Canada in 1962!

Why is this so controversial? Because many believe that pineapple does not belong on pizza (They're wrong!)
